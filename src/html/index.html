Medical services

Here are the ways we serve you

General practice

Comprehensive healthcare for individuals and families, including preventative care, chronic disease management, and health assessments

Procedures

Punch biopsies, skin cancer excisions, Implanon insertion/removal, iron infusions, venesection, lipoma excisions, and Pap smears

Mental Health

Assessment, treatment plans, and referrals for conditions such as depression, anxiety, and panic disorders

Men’s Health

Healthy heart checks, general health checks, and more

Women’s Health

Cervical screening, contraception, postnatal care, menopause management, cervical cancer screening, osteoporosis screening, smoking cessation, and alcohol cessation

Health Assessments

Over 75’s health check, people aged 45 to 49 at risk of developing a chronic disease, people aged 40 to 49 with a high risk of developing type 2 diabetes, Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander health check, intellectual disability, Dept. of Veteran’s Affairs assessments
