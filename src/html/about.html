Our reason

Why Tocumwal?

At Tocumwal Doctors, we understand the unique healthcare needs of our local community. Our commitment is to deliver high-quality medical care with a personal touch, ensuring every patient receives the best possible care in a supportive and respectful environment. Our goal is to help you achieve better health and a higher quality of life

Reliable Medical professionals

We know how important it is to have reliable medical professionals in our community. That’s why we’re dedicated to being there for you and your family, whether it’s being on-call at the local hospital, admitting patients to the acute ward, or managing nursing home care. We also provide specialised care for children and the elderly.

Our team is committed to building strong, trusting relationships with you. We want to be a part of your life and the community, ensuring you receive the attentive, compassionate care you deserve. Your health and well-being are our top priorities, and we’re here to support you every step of the way

Key resources

Need-to-know info for you

I Need My Repeat Script

  • Before issuing scripts, patients need to be reassessed to make sure that they need to continue on the same medication and the same dose. It is important that you make an appointment to see your doctor on your last repeat. Scripts will not be written without review, except in exceptional circumstances.

My Specialist Referral

  • We provide specialist referrals to enable you to get higher Medicare rebates from attending your specialist’s services. Medicare does not allow backdating referrals so please make sure to confirm with your specialist if you need a referral prior to your specialist appointment. Book an appointment with your doctor to write a referral prior to seeing your specialist

I Wish to Speak to My Doctor on the Phone

  • If you wish to speak with your doctor, we encourage you to leave a message with our reception. Your doctor will call you back when possible. It may be appropriate to book an appointment if the reason for your call is with regards to a medical condition

I Am Travelling

  • Make an appointment at least 6 weeks prior to your departure as you may need vaccinations depending on where you are travelling

Cultural Respect

  • We value you and your culture. We encourage you to let us know about your culture, especially our Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander people. We offer Health Assessments and The Closing the Gap Health Initiative in Australian health policy for Aboriginal and Torres Strait Islander peoples

I Need a Medical Certificate

  • It is important to know that a medical certificate is a legal document. A medical certificate will be issued if you present to see your doctor – we cannot issue certificates without you actually seeing the doctor

Records

  • This practice has computer-generated patient records. All patient records and details are kept strictly confidential
