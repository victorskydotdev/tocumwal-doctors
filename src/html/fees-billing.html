Fees and Billing

At Doctors@Tocumwal, we are a privately billed practice, meaning fees apply for consultations and medical services. Payment is required in full on the day of your appointment.

If you have any questions regarding fees before your visit, please speak with our reception team or your doctor.

Billing & Payments

  • Full payment is required on the day of your consultation.
  • Part payment may be considered at the discretion of the doctor and administration team in certain circumstances.

We offer on-the-spot Medicare claiming for eligible services, allowing you to claim your Medicare rebate immediately after payment.

Additional Fees

Some medical services may incur additional fees that are not covered by Medicare. These may include:

  • Nurse-assisted procedures
  • Dressings, wound care, and consumables
  • Fibreglass casts & medical supplies

These fees are charged separately from your consultation and will be discussed with you at the time of service.
